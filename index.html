About the Tutor

My Journey from
Student to Mentor

I started my trading journey in 2022, curious about the financial markets and driven by a desire for financial freedom. Like most traders, my early days were filled with losses, lessons, and a lot of screen time — but also growth.

Over the past 4 years, I've developed a deep understanding of the markets I trade: Gold (XAU/USD), Futures, and US100 CFDs. These aren't just charts to me — they're opportunities I've spent thousands of hours learning to read with precision.

My approach to trading is captured in what I call the Hail Mary philosophy — strategic, high-conviction trades built on solid analysis, disciplined risk management, and unwavering patience.

"The Hail Mary isn't about luck — it's about waiting for the right setup, trusting your analysis, and executing with conviction. Every trade is a calculated bet, not a gamble."

Today, I combine live trading with mentorship through NotesAndCoins — sharing weekly market analysis on YouTube, publishing insights on Substack, and mentoring students one-on-one through structured programs that deliver real results.
